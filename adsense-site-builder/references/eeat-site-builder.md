# E-E-A-T Site Builder

Use this reference during phase 5 to create trust, ownership, and policy pages.

## Required Pages

Create or verify:

- About
- Contact
- Privacy Policy
- Terms of Use
- Author or editorial page when content advice is substantial

## Requirements

The site must make it clear:

- Who runs the site or brand
- What the site helps users do
- How users can contact the owner
- What data the site collects
- Whether tools are informational only
- When users should verify results with a professional or official source

## Style

Write plainly. Avoid fake biographies, fake credentials, fake company claims, or unverifiable authority signals. If the owner identity is not provided, use a transparent project or brand identity and mark placeholders that need owner confirmation.

## Output

Create the pages in the site project and add a short checklist to `outputs/<site-slug>/site-brief.md`:

```md
## Trust Pages

- About: done | needs owner details
- Contact: done | needs email/form endpoint
- Privacy Policy: done | needs legal review
- Terms: done | needs legal review
- Author/editorial info: done | not applicable | needs details
```
