# AdSense Readiness Auditor

Use this reference during phase 6 before recommending AdSense submission.

## Audit Areas

Check:

- Real user utility
- Content depth and originality
- Navigation and internal links
- Trust pages
- Mobile usability
- Technical SEO
- Indexability
- AdSense policy risk
- Invalid traffic risk
- Overall site completeness

## Red Flags

Do not mark ready if:

- The site is unfinished or mostly template placeholders.
- Pages contain mostly headings, lists, or generic AI prose.
- The tool does not work or produces unclear output.
- There is no clear owner/contact/privacy information.
- Traffic plans rely on bots, traffic exchanges, paid traffic packages, or click incentives.
- The topic is heavily YMYL without appropriate caution, sourcing, or expertise.

## Output

Write `outputs/<site-slug>/adsense-readiness-report.md`:

```md
# AdSense Readiness Report

## Verdict

ready | not ready | needs revision

## Summary

## Passed Checks

## Blocking Issues

## Recommended Fixes

## Traffic Quality Notes

## Submission Recommendation
```

Use a conservative verdict. "Needs revision" is better than a risky submission.
