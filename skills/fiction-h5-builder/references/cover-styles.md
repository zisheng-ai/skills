## Genre Visual Details

### 玄幻/仙侠
- **Colors:** Blue-teal + gold + deep black; cold base with gold/warm accent
- **Characters:** Male — long tied/loose hair, sword/artifact, flowing robes. Female — fairy dress, spirit beast companion, lotus motif
- **Background:** Cloud sea, sacred mountains, ancient pagodas, spiritual energy
- **Lighting:** `divine golden light rays, mystical mist, spiritual energy glow`

### 都市
- **Colors:** Deep blue + grey + gold; neon accents (night) or warm orange (dusk)
- **Characters:** Male — suit or casual, sharp features. Female — stylish, confident
- **Background:** City skyline, high-rise offices, campus, neon streets
- **Lighting:** `sharp city lights, sunset glow reflecting on glass buildings, neon rim light`

### 古言/宫斗
- **Colors:** Imperial red + gold + ink black
- **Characters:** Female — elaborate court dress, phoenix crown, ornate makeup. Male — emperor/general
- **Background:** Palace halls, courtyards, red walls, silk curtains, lanterns
- **Lighting:** `warm lantern light, golden candle glow, silk fabric shimmering`

### 现言/甜宠
- **Colors:** Pink + warm white + soft gold
- **Characters:** Couple composition — embrace, eye contact, hand-holding
- **Background:** Café, garden, cozy interior, sunset beach
- **Lighting:** `soft warm backlighting, dreamy bokeh, gentle sunset glow`

### 悬疑/推理
- **Colors:** Black + dark grey + dark blue; blood red or cold white accent
- **Characters:** Silhouette, half-hidden face, or back view; calm or tense expression
- **Background:** Rainy night streets, old buildings, locked rooms, dark alleys
- **Lighting:** `dramatic chiaroscuro, single spotlight, rain-slicked reflections`

### 科幻/末世
- **Colors:** Deep blue + black + silver; neon blue/electric purple/energy green
- **Characters:** Mech armor / tactical suit / lab coat; sci-fi weapons, holographic interfaces
- **Background:** Space, ruined city, lab, space station
- **Lighting:** `holographic blue glow, neon rim lighting, energy arcs`

### 西幻
- **Colors:** Deep blue + dark gold + silver white; flame red/magic purple accent
- **Characters:** Knight armor / mage robes / ranger leather; dragons or griffins
- **Background:** Castles, dragon lairs, magic circles, sweeping plains
- **Lighting:** `magic spell glow, dramatic stormy sky, firelight from torches`

### 历史/军事
- **Colors:** Iron grey + dark red + earth yellow; gold armor glint/beacon fire orange
- **Characters:** General in armor / strategist in robes; wielding weapons
- **Background:** Battlefield, city walls, military camp, beacon fires
- **Lighting:** `dramatic battlefield firelight, smoke-filled sky, sunset over war`

### 灵异/恐怖
- **Colors:** Ink black + ghost green + dark red; paper white/candle yellow accent
- **Characters:** Taoist / ordinary person caught in strangeness; ghost shadows, paper figures
- **Background:** Cemetery, ancient temple, dark alley, coffins
- **Lighting:** `eerie green glow, flickering candlelight, cold ghostly luminescence`

### 轻小说/二次元
- **Colors:** Bright multi-color; star and petal accents
- **Characters:** Chibi/moe characters; cat ears, wings, fantasy accessories
- **Background:** Fantasy world, campus, isekai, starry sky
- **Lighting:** `sparkly star effects, magical particle effects, soft luminous glow`
